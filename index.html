About

I recently obtained my Bachelor's Degree in Technical Mathematics at TU Wien. Through the study I discovered my interest in the theoretical foundations of machine learning. Currently I am pursuing a Master's in Data Science.

Bachelor's Thesis

My Bachelor's thesis is about a particular approach to reinforcement learning, where the return is modelled directly by categorical distributions rather than only its expected value. I derived the sample complexity of tabular algorithms and considered the incorporation of risk measures in the action selection process.

Some Projects

MNIST from Scratch

Implementation of automatic differentiation library and testing with deep neural network on MNIST dataset in JULIA.

CartPole with RL

Reinforcement Learning in continuous state space. The task was to swingup and balance a pendulum by only applying a horizontal force to the cart. The states were approximated by a neural net.

Covid-19 Predictive Modelling

Least Square Regression based on Susceptible-Infected-Removed (SIR) model. Has no predictive value as it overfits a lot.


Simplified Snake AI with RL

First implementation of reinforcement learning algorithm for me. Here Monte Carlo methods were used to achieve the objective of collecting the red apples. In order to decrease the number of states and difficulty, the snake has no tail.


Goomo
